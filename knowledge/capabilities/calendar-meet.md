# Scheduled meeting

For a scheduled invitation, create a Calendar event with conference creation enabled. Verify event ID, attendee, timezone, start/end time, and Meet joining URL.

# Independent meeting space

Use the Meet space tool only when an independent instant joining space is explicitly requested. Conference records and participants are historical Meet resources, not Calendar invitations.
